Our timeline

2021

The company expanded its services to Germany, Austria and Switzerland. Clients outside the initial closed group were welcomed to book with ENVOY. The company introduced Smart Quotes, simplifying aircraft selection. Shortly after, expansion reached the UK and Spain.

2023

ENVOY rebranded to Hyer® Aviation. A new brand, website and tech-driven strategy were launched. A proprietary platform and membership program were introduced to support demand for exclusive flights.

2022

Shared Flights were introduced. Repatriation flights operated across Asia, Africa and Europe. Special group solutions and a carbon offset program for private aviation in Benelux were launched.

2024

Hyer developed a client portfolio and launched a mobile application for loyal clients. Increased demand led to additional sales teams and expanded flight support.
